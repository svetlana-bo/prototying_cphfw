Trends #CPHFW25

Berner Kühl

Berner Kühl believes that in today's world, where products are rapidly mass produced, we must learn to be more aware of our impact. Instead of quickly creating an item that is not built to last, Berner Kühl aims to design products the customer can buy knowing that sustainable choices were carefully made at every step of the way.

Forza Collective

Forza Collective creates functional pieces that are versatile and seasonless. The brand sources their fabrics from Limonta, this ensures that the textiles used by the Forza Collective are 100% recycled. A key aim of the brand is to create products that customers can and want to wear for a long time.

Alice K

ALICE.K is a Kyiv-based label focusing on 'basics' with extra twists. Their main objective is to celebrate life, youth and freedom. Many of the pieces produced by ALICE.K use biodegradable fabrics such as viscose (also known as rayon) and cotton.

Berner Kühl

Berner Kühl is a brand that focuses on sustainable practices. They believe that in today's world, where products are rapidly mass produced, we must learn to be more aware of our impact on the world. Instead of quickly creating an item that is not built to last, Berner Kühl aims to design products the customer can buy knowing that sustainable choices were carefully made at every step of the way.

Forza Collective

The brand uses eco-friendly materials like organic cotton and recycled synthetics, focusing on minimal environmental impact. Known for bold, gender-neutral designs, Forza Collective creates functional pieces that are versatile and seasonless.
